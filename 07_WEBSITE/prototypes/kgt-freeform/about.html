About the rally

Built for drivers, not spectators.

Korea Grand Tour is a car-first rally for people who care what they drive and want a reason to take it beyond the usual roads.

Why it works

The car gets people there. The route keeps them together.

The event creates the conditions: a starting grid, shared roads, staged checkpoints, photo moments, and enough freedom for each driver or team to make the weekend their own.

It is a car rally without race pressure, a road trip without feeling random, and a community weekend where the line-up is cars, cities, and people.
