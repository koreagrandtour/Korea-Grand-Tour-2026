# Korea Grand Tour 2026 Visual Direction

## Style Prompt

Bold Korean road-rally energy: real car media, strong red and blue brand contrast, white space, black automotive surfaces, sharp typography, video-first hero moments, and realistic navigation-style route interactions. The site should feel premium, punchy, social, and confident, not cute or illustrative.

## Colors

- Rally blue `#063c9d`: brand anchor, headings, navigation, trust anchors.
- KGT red `#ff3b23`: route line, CTA punch, active states, motion accents.
- Track black `#090b10`: automotive sections, overlays, route UI.
- White `#ffffff`: page background and contrast.
- Asphalt `#1b2430`: panels and secondary surfaces.
- Sun yellow `#f6ad16`: sparing highlights only.

## Typography

- Use system sans-serif with heavy, wide, uppercase display moments.
- Keep body copy friendly, clean, and easy to scan.

## Motion Rules

- Use video, route-line drawing, scroll-driven checkpoint reveals, smooth parallax, count-up stats, and sharp media entrances.
- Keep motion smooth and premium, never gimmicky.
- Provide `prefers-reduced-motion` fallbacks.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not make the page cute, childish, or illustration-led.
- Do not use purple/blue tech gradients.
- Do not use generic tourist stock-photo styling.
- Do not use soft cream as the dominant site color.
- Do not bury the 2026 route behind old full-route material.
- Do not vary CTA labels; use `Register now`.
