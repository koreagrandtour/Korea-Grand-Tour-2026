# Private Participant Area

This folder is local-only by default.

Do not commit, upload, share, paste, or expose contents unless François explicitly approves.

## Keep Here

- participant trackers
- payment trackers
- agreement trackers
- car photo trackers
- insurance trackers
- private notes
- private conversations
- local-only exports

## Never Put In Public / GitHub-Safe Files

- phone numbers
- emails
- car plate numbers
- insurance files
- driver license/passport files
- payment proof
- private Kakao messages
- signed agreement PDFs
- confidential participant notes

## Public-Safe Replacement

Use aggregate numbers only, such as:

- 13 pre-RSVPs
- 17 people
- 9 confirmed cars
- 5 deposits paid
- 5 agreements signed
- 1 car photo received

## GitHub Rule

This folder is ignored by `.gitignore`. Treat it as local-only even if the repository is private.
