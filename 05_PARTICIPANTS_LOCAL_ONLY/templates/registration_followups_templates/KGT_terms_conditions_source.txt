1. Introduction
Korea Grand Tour ("KGT") is a multi-day driving and lifestyle event created for people who share a love for cars, travel, culture, and positive human connection.
By registering for, attending, participating in, or remaining present at any part of KGT, each participant confirms that they have read, understood, and accepted these Terms & Conditions in full.
If a person does not agree to these Terms & Conditions, that person must not participate in KGT.
2. Nature of the Event
KGT is a touring event, not a racing event. It is not a speed competition, timed competition, race, drifting event, or any form of reckless driving activity.
Participants must comply with all applicable laws and regulations of the Republic of Korea.
Participants must follow road traffic laws, parking rules, toll obligations, and local restrictions.
Participants must follow instructions given by police, public authorities, venue staff, hotel staff, and emergency personnel.
Participants must follow all official KGT rules, safety guidance, schedules, and conduct instructions.
3. Eligibility
Drivers must be of legal driving age in the Republic of Korea.
Drivers must hold a valid licence legally permitting them to operate the relevant vehicle in Korea.
Participants must hold all documents required by law for the vehicle and for participation.
Participants must be physically and mentally fit to drive.
Participants must not drive under the influence of alcohol, drugs, medication, fatigue, or any condition affecting safe driving.
Participants must provide accurate and truthful registration information to KGT.
KGT reserves the right to refuse or cancel participation where information provided is false, misleading, incomplete, or where participation is considered unsafe or inconsistent with the spirit of the event.
4. Vehicle Requirements
Each participant is solely responsible for ensuring that their vehicle is safe, road-legal, properly maintained, and fully insured.
The vehicle must be legally registered.
The vehicle must have valid insurance as required by law.
The vehicle must be in sound mechanical condition.
The vehicle must satisfy any legally required inspection obligations.
The vehicle must carry any equipment legally required for operation in Korea.
The vehicle must be suitable for the event route, road conditions, and weather.
5. Participant Responsibility and Assumption of Risk

Each participant acknowledges and accepts these risks voluntarily.

12. Internal Disputes Between Participants
12.1 Internal Event Resolution
Participants are expected to resolve minor disagreements calmly, honestly, and in good faith whenever possible. If a dispute arises during or in connection with the event, the KGT team may review the matter and make an internal event-management decision for the purposes of safety, discipline, and participation status.
12.2 Internal Policy Where There Is Insufficient Proof
If both parties are first-time KGT participants and cannot resolve the issue on their own, both parties may be evicted from the event.
If one party is a returning participant from a previous KGT edition and the other is a first-time participant, the KGT team may place weight on the returning participant's prior conduct and history within KGT, and the first-time participant may be evicted from the event.
If both parties are returning participants from previous KGT editions and cannot resolve the issue on their own, both parties may be evicted from the event.
12.3 Scope of This Clause
This dispute policy applies only to KGT's internal participation, discipline, and removal decisions. It does not prevent any participant from reporting matters to the police, insurer, court, or other competent authority, and it does not determine civil or criminal liability under applicable law.
