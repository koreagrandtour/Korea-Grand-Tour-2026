# 15-Second Storyboard

## Concept

A short emotional invitation: the viewer sees a convoy already in motion and feels the pull of joining the road.

## Beat Sheet

| Time | Visual | On-screen text | Motion note |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0-3s | Cars enter a winding mountain road, road opening ahead | One road. | Slow push-in, engine rise |
| 3-7s | Wider view of a convoy across curves and mountains | Four days across Korea. | Road-following pan |
| 7-12s | Close-following convoy shot, cars moving together | Cars, friends, memories. | Speed ramp, warm light |
| 12-15s | Sunset road, cars disappearing into the distance | Korea Grand Tour 2026 | Logo/title hold, soft CTA |

## Voiceover Option

One road. Four days across Korea. Cars, friends, memories. Korea Grand Tour 2026.

## Caption Draft

Four days. One convoy. The kind of road you remember long after the engine is off.

Korea Grand Tour 2026.

## Hashtag Draft

#KoreaGrandTour #KoreaGrandTour2026 #KoreaRoadTrip #CarTour #DriveKorea #SupercarTour #RoadTripKorea

## Music Direction

Modern cinematic electronic track with a warm build, clean percussion, and a drop around 7 seconds for the convoy moment. Avoid aggressive racing energy; this should feel premium, emotional, and social.
