# KGT 15s Winding Roads Video Design

## Style Prompt

Cinematic Korean road-tour energy: premium but human, warm late-afternoon light, winding roads, cars moving together, direct typography, white text with Pungent Blue and Strong Orange accents.

## Colors

- Ink: `#0B1017`
- White: `#F8F8F5`
- Pungent Blue: `#0057B8`
- Strong Orange: `#FF6A00`
- Road Grey: `#2B3037`

## Typography

- Primary: system sans-serif / Arial fallback
- Weight: bold for short statements, regular for notes

## What NOT To Do

- Do not show specific unrevealed route detail.
- Do not show participant identity.
- Do not use readable license plates.
- Do not make it corporate or brochure-like.
- Do not overfill the frame with text.
