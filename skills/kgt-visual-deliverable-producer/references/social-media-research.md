# Social Media Research Workflow

Use for Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Stories, Reels, captions, hashtags, music/audio, and social growth planning.

## Research Order

1. KGT account: use the Korea Grand Tour Instagram Graph plugin when available to review recent media, captions, formats, and insights. Do not publish unless explicitly approved.
2. Similar channels: research Korean and global car/rally/road-trip/motorsport/tourism accounts.
3. Platform trends: search Instagram, YouTube, Reddit, and relevant Korean/community sources for current hooks, formats, audio/music, comments, and audience language.
4. Keyword/hashtag scan: choose few targeted hashtags/keywords for discovery and context, not spam.
5. Audience fit: prioritize car culture, Korea travel, roads, friendship, premium adventure, safety, and memory.

## Current Principles

- Instagram has multiple ranking systems by surface; Reels, Feed, Stories, Explore, and Search should not be treated as one algorithm.
- Reels discovery depends heavily on viewer activity, interaction, Reel information, visuals/audio, popularity, and creator/account signals.
- Hashtags help classification/search, but do not rescue weak content. Prefer a few precise tags.
- YouTube Shorts are ranked by viewer personalization and performance signals such as viewed vs ignored, average view duration, average percent viewed, likes/dislikes, and viewer satisfaction.
- YouTube sounds, hashtags, and trends can help discovery when they fit the content.
- Community advice is useful for pattern spotting, not as truth. Prefer official sources for ranking, eligibility, copyright, and safety.

## KGT Social Output Checklist

- Hook in first seconds/first line.
- One clear story: car, road, place, people, or reveal.
- Respect route reveal boundaries.
- Use car identity only unless participant identity is approved.
- Caption includes natural English and Korean when useful.
- Hashtags are focused: brand + car/route/Korea/travel/motorsport niche.
- Music/audio note explains mood and why it fits.
- CTA is soft and direct: register, follow, save, share, or wait for reveal.
