# Key Positioning Lines

Last updated: 2026-05-27

Use these as a copy bank. Adapt to the channel and keep claims factual.

## Core Lines

- Korea's most unique multi-day road rally and road-trip experience.
- Korea Grand Tour is a multi-day rally across South Korea, built around cars, people, culture, and unforgettable memories.
- Not just a drive.
- A journey to rediscover Korea through roads, stories, places, and people.
- A road rally built on cars, people, culture, and unforgettable memories.
- The most dynamic and human way to experience Korea.

## 2026 Lines

- Korea Grand Tour 2026 returns for its second edition from August 14 to 17.
- This year, the journey goes deep into inland Korea: valleys, mountain roads, villages, farms, cultural sites, and some of the most exciting driving roads in the country.
- Four days. Around 1,500 km. Seoul to Yangyang.
- Selected drivers, real cars, real roads, and a side of Korea most people never experience.
- The 2026 edition is more structured, more selective, and still built around the same human spirit.

## Participant Recruitment Lines

- Limited places. Selected drivers. A road trip you will remember.
- Bring the right car, the right attitude, and the curiosity to see Korea differently.
- This is for people who want more than a weekend drive.
- Cars bring us together. The road does the rest.

## Sponsor Lines

- Korea Grand Tour gives brands real-world exposure through cars, travel, content, and community.
- Products are not only displayed; they are used in a real multi-day road experience.
- Sponsor reporting should separate 2025 actuals from 2026 projections.

## Avoid

- Do not call KGT a race.
- Do not overuse fake luxury language.
- Do not present projections as actuals.
- Do not say sponsors are confirmed unless they are confirmed.
- Do not use `Registered Car` on car line-up visuals.
- Avoid `KGT` in public visual titles by default; use `Korea Grand Tour`.
